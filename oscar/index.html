About

I am a student at Boston University, where I am pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Management Studies. I have advanced knowledge in public relations, digital and emerging media technologies, corporate, consumer, and crisis communications, and digital marketing

I have more than five years of experience in strategic leadership, creative development, implementation, and evaluation of successful communications, marketing, and PR campaigns. In doing so, I have been fortunate to work with restaurants, multinational e-commerce companies, and major global consumer goods companies.

I currently work in the Marketing & Communications department at Commonwealth Corporation, where I lead a workforce development event series that has brought together over two hundred business owners from historically and systemically marginalized communities.

I was born in Mexico City, Mexico, where I studied at CESSA Universidad, and now live on the East Coast in Boston, MA. When I am not studying or working, I travel to discover new cities, countries, or restaurants. I like to cook, go scuba diving, and play strategy board games with my friends and family.

I am always looking for new projects to collaborate on or new people to meet. Explore my website to learn more about my experiences and get in touch!
