About

After earning a bachelor's degree in management studies from Boston University, I've transitioned into pursuing a master's degree in integrated marketing communications at Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism, Media & Integrated Marketing Communications.

My career encompasses over six years of experience in strategic leadership, during which I've developed, implemented, and evaluated communications, marketing, and public relations campaigns. My experience cuts across diverse industries, including the public sector, educational services, consumer goods, e-commerce, environmental services, and hospitality.

I'm originally from Mexico City, Mexico, and a proud alum of CESSA University; now, my journey has led me to Chicago. My global experience has shaped my perspective and approach to work and life, a fact reflected in my enduring love for travel and discovering new cuisines.

While I delve deeper into my studies, I am open to collaborating on new projects and networking opportunities. Please explore my website to learn more about my background, and feel free to contact me!
